Younique is a beauty products company that was established in 2012 by brother and sister Derek Maxfield and Melanie Huscroft. Younique's mission is to uplift, empower, and validate women across the globe. Younique donates 100% of net profit to help cuases around the world.


Younique produces several product types in the makeup and skin care categories. These include but are not limited to products for eye lashes, eyeliner, eyeshadow, eye primer, and tools like eye brushes. Younique also provides product for the face such as BB cream, foundation, concelear, and brushes. There are also types of lip liner, lipstick, lip gloss and lip stain. Younique has skin care as well called the You-ology line which includes toners, masks, and cleansers.
