Yu Younique TopFoxx L`ange

TopFoxx was founded by Helen Getts in 2016. A new York City company that designs unique sunglasses and accessories. TopFoxx is 100% women owned and all products are all hand assembled. In 2019 TopFoxx donated proceeds to the Younique Foundation. In 2020 donated over 10k+ meals to Food Bank for New York City. Also in 2020 TopFoxx donated proceeds to both Black Girls Code and the Trevor Project.


TopFoxx creates a assortment of sunglasses for women by women. Sunglasses have polycarbonated material and revo coated lenses. Products come in standard form or perscription form. There are also faded lenses, polarized, mirrored, and mood boosting lenses. You can find product in the following shpaes: Oval, Round, Square, and Diamond.

FDA certified shatter resistant lenses and frames are hand assembled. There are also products that are made from plants, recycled plastics, and themselves have recyclable materials. Innovative technology makes the frames lightweight and the ability to float in water. Materials are even BPA free.
